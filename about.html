Who We Are

Our Story

Two physicians. One dream. A preschool built on the belief that every child deserves to be truly known, understood, and celebrated.

How It Began

A School Built by Two Physicians Who Believed More Was Possible

Smart Beginnings Academy was co-founded by Dr. Roy Lingam and Dr. Sinthuja (Cindy) Magaswaran, two physicians who believed that the earliest years of a child's life deserve the same level of care, expertise, and intention as the best medical practice.

Their vision was clear: a school where every child is truly known, and where learning adapts to the child — not the other way around. Where warmth and science meet. Where parents feel not just informed, but genuinely partnered with in their child's growth.

From that vision, Smart Beginnings Academy was born — and today serves families across Oswego, Ogden, and Kirk & Butterfield in the greater Chicago area.

Meet the Founders

The Physicians Behind the Vision

Their medical training taught them to listen carefully, think holistically, and put the person first. They brought that same approach to early childhood education.

Dr. Roy Lingam, M.D.

Co-Founder & Medical Director

Dr. Roy Lingam brings his passion for evidence-based medicine and holistic child development to every aspect of Smart Beginnings Academy. As a practicing physician, Dr. Lingam recognized the critical importance of the first five years — not just medically, but developmentally, emotionally, and socially.

He co-founded Smart Beginnings to ensure that the children in his community received the kind of thoughtful, individualized care that he believed every child deserves — care that is as intentional as the best medical practice.

Physician Child Development Advocate Co-Founder

Dr. Sinthuja Magaswaran, M.D.

Co-Founder & Education Director

Dr. Sinthuja Magaswaran, warmly known as Dr. Cindy, is the heart and curriculum visionary of Smart Beginnings Academy. Her medical background, combined with a deep understanding of pediatric development, shapes every program and policy at the school.

Dr. Cindy's approach is rooted in compassion and curiosity. She believes that a child who feels safe, seen, and loved will always find their way to learning — and that it is the teacher's job to build that bridge, not the child's job to cross it alone.

Physician Curriculum Visionary Co-Founder
Our Values

What Guides Everything We Do

Every Child Matters

Not every child in the class — each individual child. We know their names, their quirks, their strengths, and how they learn best.

Safety Is Non-Negotiable

As physicians, we hold safety to medical standards. Every protocol exists for one reason: your child's wellbeing.

Curiosity Is Sacred

We never stifle a question. Curiosity is the engine of learning, and we feed it with joy, creativity, and encouragement every day.

Families Are Partners

We don't just care for your child — we walk alongside your family. Daily updates, open communication, and genuine partnership.

Excellence Is the Baseline

Good enough isn't good enough. As physicians, we hold ourselves to a standard of excellence in everything — and our school reflects that.

Community First

We believe schools build neighborhoods. Smart Beginnings is rooted in the community we serve and committed to giving back to it.
