Preschool Program

Where curiosity turns into confidence. Our preschool program blends structured learning and imaginative play to build the foundational skills every child needs for kindergarten success.

OUR CURRICULUM

Six Pillars of Preschool Learning

Our physician-informed curriculum addresses the whole child — cognitive, social, emotional, and physical development.

📚

Early Literacy

Phonemic awareness, letter recognition, storytime, and writing readiness through daily reading and language-rich interactions.

🔢

Math Foundations

Counting, sorting, patterns, and shapes through hands-on manipulatives, games, and real-world problem-solving activities.

🎨

Creative Arts

Painting, drawing, music, and dramatic play to cultivate self-expression, fine motor skills, and creative thinking.

🔬

Science & Discovery

Simple experiments, nature exploration, and observation journals to build a love of asking "why" and figuring out "how."

🤝

Social-Emotional Learning

Identifying feelings, conflict resolution, empathy, and friendship skills — the building blocks of emotional intelligence.

🌳

Outdoor Learning

Daily outdoor time for gross motor development, nature immersion, and unstructured play proven to support cognitive growth.

A TYPICAL DAY

Structure That Sparks Learning

6:30 – 9:00 AM
Arrival & Morning Choice Time
Self-directed play at learning stations: blocks, dramatic play, puzzles, and books
9:00 – 9:45 AM
Morning Circle & Group Lesson
Calendar, weather, theme introduction, shared reading, and group discussion
9:45 – 11:15 AM
Learning Centers & Small Groups
Rotating activity stations: literacy, math, science, art, and sensory play
11:15 AM – 12:15 PM
Outdoor Play
Structured outdoor games plus free play on our age-appropriate playground
12:15 – 2:30 PM
Lunch & Rest
Hot nutritious lunch, quiet rest time or independent reading for non-sleepers
2:30 – 6:00 PM
Afternoon Enrichment & Pickup
Snack, additional projects, story time, and relaxed afternoon play
WHAT THEY'LL GAIN

Kindergarten-Ready Skills

Children who complete our preschool program enter kindergarten with confidence, curiosity, and essential skills across all domains.

  • ✓Recognizes letters and beginning sounds — building blocks of early reading readiness
  • ✓Counts to 20 and understands basic number concepts — sorting, comparing, and simple patterns
  • ✓Follows multi-step directions and can work independently for 10–15 minutes
  • ✓Manages transitions and classroom routines with confidence and minimal support
  • ✓Strong fine motor skills — pencil grip, scissors, and handwriting readiness
  • ✓Healthy friendships and conflict resolution skills — communicates needs and feelings appropriately
  • ✓Daily ProCare report — parents see learning activities, meals, and highlights every day
👩‍🏫

Our Preschool Teachers

  • ✓Bachelor's or Associate's in Early Childhood Education
  • ✓Illinois State licensed — DCFS compliant
  • ✓CPR & first aid certified — renewed annually
  • ✓Trained in literacy and numeracy best practices
  • ✓Ongoing professional development in child development
