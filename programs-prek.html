Pre-K Program

The final chapter before kindergarten. Our Pre-K program provides an academically rich, joyful experience that prepares children to walk through those school doors with knowledge, confidence, and heart.

PRE-K CURRICULUM

Built for Kindergarten Success

Advanced academics paired with deep social-emotional development — because school readiness is about the whole child.

📖

Advanced Literacy

Blending sounds, sight words, writing sentences, and comprehension strategies to build confident early readers.

🧮

Math & Logic

Addition concepts, measurement, geometry, and logical sequencing through hands-on games and problem-solving challenges.

🚀

STEM Exploration

Simple engineering challenges, coding fundamentals, science experiments, and technology integration to spark a love of discovery.

🎭

Creative Arts & Drama

Advanced projects, storytelling, performance, and collaborative art — building creativity and communication skills together.

💪

Leadership & Character

Peer leadership projects, responsibility, integrity, and community-mindedness to build children of character.

🌐

Social Studies & World Awareness

Community helpers, cultural celebrations, maps, and an introduction to the wider world beyond their classroom.

A TYPICAL DAY

A Full Day of Learning

6:30 – 9:00 AM
Arrival & Independent Learning Centers
Self-directed literacy, math, building, and creative play while all friends arrive
9:00 – 10:00 AM
Morning Meeting & Literacy Block
Calendar, news sharing, phonics, shared reading, and writing workshop
10:00 – 11:00 AM
Math & STEM Exploration
Hands-on math manipulatives, engineering challenges, and science experiments
11:00 AM – 12:15 PM
Outdoor Play & Gross Motor
Structured outdoor games, cooperative play, and physical education activities
12:15 – 2:30 PM
Lunch & Rest / Independent Reading
Nutritious lunch, quiet time — older Pre-K students may do independent book reading
2:30 – 6:00 PM
Creative Arts, Projects & Pickup
Afternoon enrichment, project-based learning, creative expression, and family pickup
GRADUATION BENCHMARKS

What Pre-K Graduates Can Do

Our Pre-K completers consistently exceed kindergarten readiness benchmarks across Illinois school districts.

  • ✓Read simple words and sentences — solid phonics foundation and sight word vocabulary
  • ✓Write their full name and simple sentences with correct pencil grip and letter formation
  • ✓Count to 100, add and subtract within 10, and understand basic place value
  • ✓Work independently for 20+ minutes and follow multi-step classroom routines without reminders
  • ✓Navigate peer conflict independently — express feelings, listen, and find solutions
  • ✓Demonstrate a growth mindset — embrace challenge, persist through difficulty, and celebrate others
  • ✓ProCare portfolio — parents receive daily updates and can review their child's full-year learning journey
👩‍🏫

Our Pre-K Lead Teachers

  • ✓Bachelor's degree in Early Childhood Education or K–3 Elementary Education
  • ✓Illinois Professional Educator License (PEL)
  • ✓CPR & first aid certified — renewed annually
  • ✓Trained in Reading Recovery and early numeracy frameworks
  • ✓Continuous professional learning in kindergarten transition best practices
