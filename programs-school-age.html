Flexible Care That Fits Your Family

We offer multiple care options so your child always has a safe, supportive place to be — no matter your work schedule.

🌅

Before School Care

6:30 AM – School Start

A calm, organized morning start with breakfast, reading, and activities before the school day begins.

🌇

After School Care

School Dismissal – 6:00 PM

Snack, homework help, outdoor play, and creative enrichment activities until pickup time.

📅

Full-Day Care

School Holidays & E-Learning Days

Full-day programming on school breaks, institute days, and e-learning days so you never miss a beat at work.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

More Than Just a Safe Place to Wait

Our school-age program keeps kids engaged, growing, and happy — not just supervised.

  • ✓Homework help — dedicated quiet time with staff support for assignments
  • ✓Healthy snacks — nutritious afternoon snack provided daily
  • ✓Outdoor play — daily active play for physical well-being
  • ✓Enrichment activities — arts, crafts, STEM projects, and more
  • ✓Safe transportation coordination — we work with local schools on pickup logistics
  • ✓ProCare updates — daily digital check-ins so parents stay in the loop
