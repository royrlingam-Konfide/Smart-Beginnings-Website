WHAT THEY'LL EXPERIENCE

Growing, Learning & Exploring Together

Toddlerhood is one of the most rapid periods of brain development. Our curriculum is built around natural curiosity — turning every moment into a learning opportunity.

  • ✓Language-rich environment — constant narration, singing, and reading to build vocabulary and early communication
  • ✓Sensory play stations — tactile bins, water tables, and textured art activities to build neural connections
  • ✓Emerging independence — self-feeding, coat hooks, and clean-up routines that build pride and capability
  • ✓Gross motor development — climbing, running, and outdoor play to build coordination and strength
  • ✓Social-emotional coaching — gentle guidance through "mine!" moments and early friendship skills
  • ✓ProCare daily updates — photos, meals, diaper changes, and nap times sent directly to your phone

Rhythm & Routine

6:30 – 9:00 AM
Arrival & Morning Free Play
