Stay Connected

Seamless Daily Communication Between Parents and Educators

Through the ProCare app, parents stay connected to their child's day in real time — no guessing, no waiting until pickup.

Real-Time Photo Updates
Receive candid photos of your child learning, playing, and growing — throughout the day.
Daily Activity Reports
Meals eaten, nap times, diaper changes, and learning activities — all logged and shared instantly.
Direct Messaging with Teachers
Send a quick message to your child's teacher anytime — questions answered, milestones celebrated together.
Instant Notifications
Get notified the moment your child arrives, eats, sleeps, or has a special moment worth sharing.

Founded by Physicians

Co-founded by Dr. Roy Lingam and Dr. Cindy Magas — our medical expertise means your child's health and development always come first.

We Teach the Way They Learn

Every child receives a truly individualized approach tailored to how they think, explore, and grow — because no two learners are alike.

Parents receive daily photos and activity updates through the ProCare app — so you're never wondering how your child's day is going.

Safe & Secure Environment

Experienced, Caring Teachers

Community & Belonging
