Safety First

Health & Safety

As physicians and parents, our founders built every safety protocol with medical-grade rigor. Your child's wellbeing is never an afterthought — it is the foundation.

"As physicians, our founders built Smart Beginnings Academy with health and safety at its foundation. Every protocol, every policy, and every procedure exists for one reason: to ensure your child is safe, healthy, and thriving every single day."

— Dr. Roy Lingam & Dr. Sinthuja Magaswaran, Co-Founders

Our Protocols

Comprehensive Safety at Every Level

Six pillars of safety — each designed by medical professionals to give you total peace of mind.

Secure Facility

Our buildings are designed with your child's security as the top priority. Every point of entry is controlled and monitored throughout the day.

Keypad-secured entry at all access points

ID verification for all adult visitors

Authorized pickup lists strictly enforced

Security cameras at all entry and common areas

Staff Background Checks

Every member of our team — from teachers to administrators — undergoes comprehensive background screening before joining our staff.

State and federal criminal background checks

Child abuse and neglect registry checks

Employment history and reference verification

Ongoing training in child safety and mandated reporting

Health & Illness Policy

Our physician-designed illness policy protects all children while ensuring families have clear, compassionate guidance when their child is unwell.

Daily health screenings at drop-off

Clear symptom exclusion guidelines (physician-authored)

Immediate parent notification for any health concern

Medication administration protocols with parent authorization

Sanitation Protocols

Our cleaning standards are modeled on medical facility practices — because we believe the bar for cleanliness should be just as high in a preschool as in a clinic.

EPA-approved disinfectants used daily on all surfaces

Toy and equipment sanitation between uses

Handwashing routines reinforced throughout the day

Deep cleaning every evening by professional staff

Emergency Procedures

Every staff member is trained and prepared. Our emergency protocols are comprehensive, practiced regularly, and designed to ensure the fastest, safest response possible.

CPR and First Aid certified staff at all times

Fire, severe weather, and lockdown drills practiced regularly

Clear parent communication protocol during any incident

Allergy and medical emergency action plans on file

Nutrition & Meals

Healthy development starts with healthy eating. Our nutrition guidelines are designed by professionals to support growing minds and bodies throughout the school day.

Nutritious snacks provided; no added sugars or artificial dyes

Severe allergy awareness — nut-conscious environment

Dietary restrictions and medical needs accommodated

Hydration encouraged throughout the day
