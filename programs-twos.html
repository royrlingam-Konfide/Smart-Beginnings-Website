WHAT THEY'LL EXPERIENCE

Big Feelings, Bigger Discoveries

Two-year-olds are wired to explore — and to push boundaries. Our Twos classroom meets them exactly where they are, with patient teachers who guide them through big emotions, first friendships, and blossoming language.

  • ✓Language explosion — songs, stories, and rich conversation expand vocabularies daily
  • ✓Self-help skills — hand-washing, dressing, tidying up build real independence
  • ✓Sensory & art play — painting, water tables, playdough fuel creativity and fine-motor growth
  • ✓Social foundations — turn-taking, sharing, and first friendships blossom in a caring community
  • ✓Outdoor exploration — daily outdoor play builds gross-motor skills and healthy bodies
  • ✓ProCare daily updates — real-time photos and milestones sent straight to your phone
