What We Look For

We're clear about what great looks like

  • Warmth and reliability. Children thrive on consistency. We need educators who show up — emotionally and physically — every day.

  • Patience and adaptability. Every child is different. We look for educators who flex their approach, not their expectations.

  • A team-first mindset. You collaborate, communicate, and lift the room — not just your own corner of it.

  • Commitment to child safety. DCFS standards are our floor, not our ceiling. We take licensing, ratios, and safety seriously.

  • Professionalism with heart. We want people who are kind to families, respectful of boundaries, and proud of what they do.
