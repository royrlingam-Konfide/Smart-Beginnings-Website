Infant Program

Your baby's first experience of the world outside home deserves to be warm, safe, and filled with love. Our infant program is built around responsiveness, attachment, and the gentle nurturing your baby needs to thrive.

Schedule a Tour

What Your Baby Will Experience

  • Warm, responsive caregiving from dedicated infant specialists who genuinely love babies
  • Individualized schedules that follow your baby's natural feeding, sleeping, and wake rhythms
  • Sensory-rich activities: tummy time, high-contrast visuals, gentle music, and textured toys
  • Consistent caregiver assignments to build secure attachments
  • Language development through songs, stories, and constant loving conversation
  • Daily photos and detailed updates sent directly to parents via the ProCare app

A Typical Day for Your Infant

6:30 – 9:00 AM

Arrival, morning greetings, gentle wake-up sensory play

9:00 – 10:30 AM

Feeding, tummy time, high-contrast visual play, soft music

10:30 AM – 12:00 PM

Morning nap, followed by story time and gentle movement activities

12:00 – 2:30 PM

Feeding, social play, cause-and-effect toy exploration, afternoon nap

2:30 – 5:00 PM

Afternoon activities, feeding, outdoor air (weather permitting), relaxed play

5:00 – 6:00 PM

Wind-down, parent pickup, caregiver daily summary shared

Schedules flex to match each baby's individual needs.

Teacher Qualifications

  • • Early Childhood Education credentials
  • • Infant/Toddler development specialist training
  • • Infant CPR and First Aid certified
  • • Ongoing professional development in infant care
