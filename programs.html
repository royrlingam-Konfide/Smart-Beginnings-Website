"If a child can't learn the way we teach, we teach the way they learn."

This principle drives every program we offer. No two children learn the same way — and our classrooms are designed to honor that.

Ages 6 Weeks – 12 Months
Infants

Your Baby's First and Best Beginning

Our infant program provides the gentlest, most responsive care imaginable. Small ratios, dedicated caregivers, and a warm environment ensure your baby feels safe, stimulated, and loved from their very first day.

  • Low caregiver-to-infant ratios (1:3 or better)
  • Individualized schedules following your baby's natural rhythms
  • Daily photos and updates via ProCare app
  • Sensory-rich, developmentally appropriate activities
Learn More
Toddlers

Curiosity Unleashed, Safely Guided

Toddlers are explorers by nature. Our program channels that boundless energy into purposeful play, early language development, and the first steps toward social interaction and independence.

  • Language-rich environment to support rapid vocabulary growth
  • Fine and gross motor skill development through active play
  • Introduction to sharing, turn-taking, and empathy
  • Consistent routines that build confidence and security

Ages 1 – 2 Years
Age 3 Years
Preschool

Where Learning Becomes an Adventure

Three-year-olds are ready for the magic of learning together. Our preschool program blends creative play with foundational academics in an environment that celebrates every small win.

  • Pre-reading and early phonics introduction
  • Number sense, shapes, and early math concepts
  • Art, music, and creative self-expression
  • Emotional regulation and conflict resolution skills
Learn More
Pre-K

Ready for Kindergarten. Ready for Life.

Our Pre-K program is the bridge between early childhood and elementary school. Children leave Smart Beginnings not just academically prepared, but emotionally confident, socially skilled, and genuinely excited to learn.

  • Reading readiness and letter/word recognition
  • Math foundations: counting, addition, patterns
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving projects
  • Self-advocacy, independence, and executive function skills
