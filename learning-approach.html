What is IELS? Illinois Early Learning Standards

The Illinois Early Learning and Development Standards (IELS) are the state's official guidelines for what children should know and be able to do from birth through kindergarten. Think of them as a research-backed roadmap — covering five key areas of development: how children learn, physical growth, language and literacy, thinking and reasoning, and social-emotional health. Every activity and lesson at Smart Beginnings is intentionally designed around these standards, so you can be confident your child is building the right foundations at exactly the right time.
