Every camp week is packed with activities, community, and care — so parents can work with total peace of mind.

  • ✓Weekly themed activities — new curriculum every week keeps kids engaged all summer
  • ✓Outdoor water play — splash days and outdoor activities all summer long

  • ✓Flexible weekly enrollment — enroll by the week to fit your family's schedule
  • ✓ProCare daily photos — share the summer memories as they happen
